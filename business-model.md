# Business Model   
|                                                                     **Department / Vertical** |                                  **Functions** |                                         **Key Responsibilities** |                                           **Example KPIs / Metrics** |
|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------|
|                                                                      **Executive & Strategy** |         Vision, leadership, strategic planning | Define goals, manage cross-department coordination, drive growth | Revenue growth %, strategic initiative completion, company valuation |
|                                         **[Finance & Accounting](finance-and-accounting.md)** |              Budgeting, forecasting, reporting |          Manage cash flow, prepare statements, ensure compliance |        Net profit margin, cash flow ratio, audit accuracy, burn rate |
|                     **[Human Resources (HR) / People Ops](human-resources-hr-people-ops.md)** |                 Recruitment, training, culture | Hire/manage employees, oversee payroll/benefits, maintain morale |               Employee turnover rate, time-to-hire, engagement score |
|                                                                          **Engineering / IT** |         Product and infrastructure development |     Build and maintain products, ensure tech uptime and security |           System uptime %, deployment frequency, bug resolution time |
|                                                               **Product Management & Design** |                Product vision, user experience |           Define roadmap, conduct user testing, manage lifecycle |                           Feature adoption rate, NPS, time-to-market |
|                                             **[Marketing & Growth](marketing-and-growth.md)** | Branding, customer acquisition, communications |                 Manage campaigns, SEO/SEM, content, and lead gen |          CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), ROAS, lead conversion rate |
|                                                              **Sales & Business Development** |                       Revenue and partnerships |              Identify leads, close deals, manage client accounts |                     Sales growth %, deal win rate, average deal size |
|                                 **[Customer Support / Success](customer-support-success.md)** |                     Retention and satisfaction |                   Handle queries, onboard users, gather feedback |                            Customer retention rate, CSAT, churn rate |
|                               **[Operations / Administration](operations-administration.md)** |             Process optimization and logistics |             Manage day-to-day operations, vendors, and processes |                  Process efficiency, cost savings %, turnaround time |
|                                                 **[Data & Analytics](data-and-analytics.md)** |                  Insights and decision support |               Collect, analyze, and visualize data for all teams |             Report accuracy, time to insight, decision adoption rate |
|                                                                        **Legal & Compliance** |                   Risk and contract management |        Handle contracts, ensure regulatory compliance, manage IP |          Contract turnaround time, compliance incidents, legal costs |
|                                                                          **R&D / Innovation** |         New technology and product exploration |             Prototype new solutions, conduct feasibility studies |                         Number of prototypes, patents filed, R&D ROI |
| **[Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) / ESG](corporate-social-responsibility-csr-esg.md)** |                      Sustainability and ethics |        Implement social/environmental initiatives, report impact |         ESG score, sustainability rating, community engagement index |
